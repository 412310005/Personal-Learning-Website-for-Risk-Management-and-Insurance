Site banner (optional)
-----------------------
Place ONE of these files in this folder:

  banner.jpg
  banner.png
  banner.webp

They will appear:
  - At the top of index.html (Vercel static page)
  - Below the main title in the Streamlit app (app.py)

Recommended: wide image (e.g. 1200x400), under ~500 KB for fast load.
